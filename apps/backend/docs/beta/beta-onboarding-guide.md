# 🌊 FISH_MARKET Beta Onboarding Guide

Welcome to the FISH_MARKET Beta Program! Your feedback is critical to our successful launch.

## 🚀 Getting Started / தொடங்குதல்
1. **Access the Beta App**: Visit [beta.fishmarket.app](https://beta.fishmarket.app)
2. **Enter Invite Code**: Use the unique 6-digit code sent to you via WhatsApp.
   - *உங்கள் வாட்ஸ்அப்பிற்கு அனுப்பப்பட்ட 6 இலக்க குறியீட்டை உள்ளிடவும்.*
3. **Setup Profile**: Complete your name and boat/market details.

## 📝 Your Mission / உங்கள் பணி
Test the app in your real environment (harbor, market, or office). Use the **Feedback Button** (blue checkmark 💬) on the bottom right to report:
- 🐞 **Bugs**: Something isn't working.
- 💡 **Ideas**: Something could be better.
- 🗣️ **Voice**: Test the Tamil voice input in noisy areas!

## 📞 Support / உதவி
If you get stuck, call us or message the Beta WhatsApp Group anytime.
